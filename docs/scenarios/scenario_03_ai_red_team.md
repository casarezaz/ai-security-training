# Scenario 3: Building an AI Red Team Program

## Situation

A Fortune 500 company has 15+ AI/ML models in production across fraud detection, customer recommendations, content moderation, and internal automation. The CISO wants to establish a formal AI Red Team that continuously tests these models for security vulnerabilities, bias, and reliability failures.

## What Makes This Hard

Each model has a different architecture, data pipeline, and threat profile. Red teaming an image classifier requires different skills than red teaming an LLM. The team needs to balance thoroughness with velocity — they can't spend months on each model. They also need to red team without disrupting production systems or creating regulatory incidents.

## Your Mission

Design the AI Red Team program: team structure and required skills, assessment methodology per model type (traditional ML, computer vision, NLP, LLM), tooling and automation requirements, reporting format for different stakeholders (CISO, model owners, legal/compliance), cadence and prioritization framework.

## Success Criteria

A program charter that the CISO can present to the board, with year-one budget estimate, staffing plan, assessment roadmap, and measurable success criteria.
